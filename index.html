Our Story


South Balcony is a real estate consultant platform where we help you in your journey to find the ideal property. Not only do we offer both buying and renting options but also aid you in searching for spaces with respect to your taste, preferred amenities, choice of locality and budget. Besides residential apartments, we also look after helping you to locate the right property for commercial spaces like corporate offices, nursing homes, etc. South Balcony is a trusted place where we can help you to find the right home that you find suitable for yourself. In addition to all these, our real estate consultant platform can also help you in arranging for property loans. All of our services are now available across the different regions of Kolkata. South Balcony aims to become one of the most trusted and reliable real estate consultants by providing exceptional overviews of the property that you choose to buy or rent.
